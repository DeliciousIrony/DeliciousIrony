Stay in the Loop

It's not much fun to be in a new town or city and find yourself in the dark about the fun and exciting events happening near you.

Do you have an event or social gathering of your own that you would like to invite others to? Getting the message out about your event can be difficult.

EventLoop is a mobile application that makes it easy to learn about Events as well as to publicize about your own Events.

How it Works

Log into the EventLoop application with your Facebook account.

After a successful login, you will be redirected to a list of all Events that have been submitted to EventLoop in the last 12 hours.

EventLoop will use the GPS functionality of your mobile device to display your distance from each Event.

To publicize your own event to other EventLoop users, click the 'Create New Event' button and you will receive a popup to describe your event. Be sure to provide some information on the event as well as its address/location details.

At the time of Event Submission, the GPS coordinates of your mobile device will be associated with your event, allowing other users to see their distance from your Event.

Feel free to upvote or downvote other events based upon your experience at them.
